Background and Motivation

Dishonest Charts

  • Visualizations can convey a misleading impression.
  • Occur by accident or design.
  • Data is always biased and never objective or neutral.
  • Result from design choice or data manipulation.
  • Effects on interpretation:
    • Patterns
    • Trends
    • Relationships

9 Dishonest Chart Techniques

  • Cherry Picking
  • Underbinning
  • Axis Truncation
  • Time Gap
  • Oversmoothing
  • Artificial Flattening or Steepening
  • Manipulating the Visual Encoding
  • Perspective Distortion
  • Concealing Uncertainty
